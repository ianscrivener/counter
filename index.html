Calculation Method

Male Suicides:

  • Based on 2,455 male suicides in 2022 (ABS data)
  • Daily rate: 6.7 deaths per day
  • Counter increment: 1 death every 12,896 seconds

Male Early Deaths:

  • Based on 17,960 male potentially avoidable deaths before age 75 in 2022 (AIHW data)
  • Daily rate: 49.2 deaths per day
  • Counter increment: 1 death every 1,756 seconds

Start Date: May 21, 2022 00:00:00 UTC

Definitions:

  • Suicides: Deaths classified under ICD-10 codes X60-X84 and Y87.0
  • Early deaths: Potentially avoidable deaths in males before age 75, as defined by AIHW methodology

All calculations use official Australian government mortality statistics and represent statistical averages based on the most recent complete annual data available.

Purpose

This counter serves as a stark reminder of the ongoing mental health crisis affecting Australian men. By displaying real-time statistics, we aim to:

  • Raise awareness about male suicide rates in Australia
  • Highlight the urgency of mental health support and intervention
  • Encourage conversations about men's mental health
  • Advocate for better support systems and resources

The Crisis

Australia has one of the highest suicide rates among developed nations, with men accounting for approximately 75% of all suicide deaths. This represents not just statistics, but real lives, families, and communities affected by this preventable tragedy.

Getting Help

Crisis Support

  • Lifeline: 13 11 14 (24/7 crisis support)
  • Beyond Blue: 1300 22 4636 (24/7 support line)
  • MensLine Australia: 1300 78 99 78 (24/7 telephone and online support)
  • Emergency: 000

Take Action

If you or someone you know is struggling:

  • Reach out to mental health services
  • Talk to friends, family, or healthcare professionals
  • Support mental health organizations and advocacy
  • Help reduce stigma by having open conversations

This website is for awareness purposes only and is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you are in crisis, please contact emergency services or a crisis helpline immediately.
